Controls
  • Move: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Jump: W / Up / Space
  • Build: left click place, right click erase (drag to erase smoothly)
  • Undo / redo: Ctrl+Z · Ctrl+Y or Ctrl+Shift+Z
  • Copy region: Alt-drag to select, then Ctrl+C or Copy region · Ctrl+V or Paste at cursor
  • Labels: Add text, then click a cell (saved with level JSON)
  • Test spawn: Shift+click a solid tile — Play starts there (your editor level only)
  • Pan map: mouse wheel, middle-drag, or arrow keys (build mode); two-finger drag on touch
Core rule
Build mode hides sabotage. When you hit Play, every placed tile “activates” a secret behavior for this run. Place exactly one Checkpoint — touch it in play for one revive per run; the next lethal hit ends the run.

Rules & Tips
- Your difficulty score is based on placed tiles (platform 1, spike 2, jump pad 1.5, hex 2.5).
- Beating higher difficulty levels grants a bonus multiplier to points earned.
- The leaderboard is local to this browser only (per your players).
- Sabotage is consistent per run: it won’t “reroll” mid-run, but it can trigger on timers.
